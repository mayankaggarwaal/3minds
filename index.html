How it works

Three AI agents collaborate in structured cycles, each with a distinct role and perspective.

🧠

Solver

Proposes a solution on cycle 1. On later cycles, revises it based on the Critic's feedback and Validator's score.

🔍

Critic

Rigorously challenges the Solver. Surfaces weaknesses, missing edge cases, and concrete improvement suggestions.

⚖️

Validator

Scores the solution 0-10, decides approved / needs revision / rejected, and writes the final answer.

🔄

Cycles

Each cycle the solution improves. If approved early, the pipeline stops. All history is preserved.
